Cognitive Defense & Photogrammetry

Headquartered in Boulder, Colorado, our Cognitive Defense division represents a longstanding, highly specialized R&D partnership between SkyGuard Defense Systems and our software engineering partners at InSitu Labs. Born in the lab to solve complex spatial anomalies, our proprietary suite of computer vision and photogrammetry tools translates raw optical data into actionable, kinetic defense.

We don't just build physical barriers; we engineer real-world software solutions that interface seamlessly with standard Robot Operating System (ROS) architectures and proprietary guidance hardware. By giving autonomous systems the ability to mathematically verify structural geometry in real-time, we neutralize optical spoofing tactics at the code level.
