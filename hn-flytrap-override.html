Show HN: Bypassing optical spoofing (DPA/Flytrap) with live spatial skeletonization

By SkyGuard_Lead | SkyGuard Defense Systems (InSitu Labs) | Denver, CO

Modern Autonomous Target Tracking (ATT) matrices are fundamentally flawed. If your interceptor relies on surface-level photon reflection—whether it is parsing RGB, tracking HSV/HSL contours, relying on YCbCr video transmission, or even utilizing shallow Near-Infrared (NIR)—it is operating on a vulnerable medium. It can be blinded, tricked, or hijacked by 2D projections and geometric noise.

The current iteration of this vulnerability is the printed "Flytrap" adversarial umbrella, a Distance-Pulling Attack (DPA) designed to spoof standard optical sensors. The standard drone hovers, confused. Tracking boxes flicker randomly. The gimbal twitches. The intercept fails.

But the Flytrap is just the baseline. Tomorrow, adversaries will deploy dynamic E-ink camouflage, localized holographic noise, and adaptive multispectral cloaking.

At SkyGuard, we bypass the visual feed entirely. We are completely agnostic to the illusion. We don't care what spectrum an adversary broadcasts in, or what colorspace a legacy camera uses to interpret it. If it occupies physical volume, we isolate the math and we lock on.

The O.T.I.S. Override Pipeline

To achieve the INTERCEPT LOCK you see in the override window, we push the sensor data through a deterministic, four-stage pipeline:

1. Spectral Delamination Matrix (SDM)

A color-separation and multispectral decoupling node we built to aggressively strip away optical camouflage, high-contrast spoofing patterns, and adversarial noise across all standard and non-standard color spaces (RGB, HSV, YUV, NIR). It renders the illusion mathematically inert.

2. Kinetic Contour Mapping (KCM)

Once the spectral noise is stripped, KCM initiates a dynamic edge-detection protocol. It utilizes topological light rendering to instantly separate true physical edges from projected shadows or 2D illusions.

3. Volumetric Stereo-Triangulation (VST)

This is where we break the DPA and future-proof the intercept. VST is our live depth-perception engine that generates an un-spoofable spatial mesh. By calculating depth continuously, distance-manipulation tactics become useless.

Our disparity calculation minimizes spatial error by enforcing structural smoothness while aggressively preserving the hard boundaries identified by KCM:

$$E(\mathbf{p}) = \sum_{\mathbf{q} \in \mathcal{N}(\mathbf{p})} w_{\mathbf{q}} \left\| I_L(\mathbf{q}) - I_R(\mathbf{q} - \mathbf{d}(\mathbf{p})) \right\|^2 + \lambda \|\nabla \mathbf{d}(\mathbf{p})\|^2$$

4. Structural Skeletonization (The "X-Ray" Override)

The final stage. O.T.I.S. renders the target down to its raw geometric wireframe. The spoofed exterior disappears entirely from the tracking matrix. The system locks a red wireframe precisely onto the true center mass to calculate a zero-latency kinetic intercept.
